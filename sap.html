The Beginning

In 2011, I was part of SAP Labs China as a Design Intern in Shanghai. It was my first step to go to the B2B industry and exposed the industry design experience at enterprise level.

I was worked with development team and product managers to build a data visualization platform that helped the business analyst to export report swiftly and efficiently.

The development team applied the scrum method which there has a scrum master to control all the session and stand-up meeting each morning and plan meeting every week. I was one of them to join in the group to add the design improvement into their development progress.
